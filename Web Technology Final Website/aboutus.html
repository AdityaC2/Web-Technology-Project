About Us

Our main aim is to curb the poverty rate in the backward areas of India.

In a world where so much is being done for technology and so little for the Society, we present to you a small initiative from our side with a mission to help those living in poverty.

We as an NGO believe that by taking small steps towards this big issue as a team would help our country to grow in all aspects.

This website spreads awareness on the issue of poverty in the backward areas of India, along with the discussion of issues such as finacial inequality and the lack of opportunities present for those in poverty. Moreover, it enocurages the general public to contribute to the cause by suggesting solutions and recommendations that would help reduce the rate of poverty in India.
